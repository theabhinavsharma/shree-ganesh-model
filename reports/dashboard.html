The verdict explained — why "LONG-HORIZON BASKET" today

Every weekday at 18:00 IST, the system pulls fresh data from 14 sources and re-trains the model. It then asks three questions per stock:

  1. Will it spike +5% within the next 7 days?
  2. Will it move +10% within 15 days?
  3. Will it double in 180 days?

Each answer is a calibrated probability. 0.95 means a verified 95% real hit rate (not just model confidence — actually checked against 6,000+ OOS samples).

Today, the highest single-stock score is 0.68. No name reached the 0.95 gold-band, but 8 names cleared the 0.86 multibagger bar (also 90% calibrated, longer horizon).

Why trust the calibration?

"Calibrated" means: when the model says 0.80, it's right 80% of the time in real OOS data. Not just claims it.

What can the system actually predict? (achievable frontier)

The frontier search tested 70 (return %, hold days) combos. Verdict:

  • 2-7% in 3-15 days at 90%+ confidence → ✅ achievable, fires regularly
  • 10% in 15+ days at 90%+ confidence → ✅ achievable
  • 15% in 30+ days at 90%+ confidence → ✅ achievable
  • 20%+ in any horizon at 90%+ confidence → ❌ structural ceiling, model can't reach
  • 100% in 180+ days at 90%+ confidence → ✅ this is the multibagger basket above
